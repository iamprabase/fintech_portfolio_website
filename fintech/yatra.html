Personalized Card

Personalized card is suitable for regular commuters who want to use its wallet or bank account for recharging the card as required. It can be value of maximum Rs. 25000.00 as approved by the Central Bank of Nepal. This card can us recharged with bank account or wallet account. This card contains Detail of the card users along with Picture. The card users have to complete KYC form for getting card. This card is suitable for Students, Senior Citizens, Citizen with Different Ability and other categories of passengers who is entitled to get concession in the public transport.

Non-Personalized Card

This card is useful for the regular commuters with minimum stored value. The maximum limit that can be stored in the card is Rs. 5000.00. Fintech provides this card with certain deposit to the passengers. The card can be recharged from Fintech booth, inside the bus and other location specified by the Fintech. The card owner can recharge any value in the card. The card is valid for three years. It can be renewed before its expiry.
